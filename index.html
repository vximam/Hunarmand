Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about Hunarmand.

How do I find a skilled worker near me?
Simply post your request with your location, describe the job, and our system will match you with verified professionals in your area. You'll receive quotes within minutes.
Are the workers on Hunarmand verified?
Yes. Every worker goes through a strict verification process including ID check, skill assessment, and background screening before they can accept jobs on our platform.
How does the payment work?
Payments are held securely by Hunarmand and only released to the worker after you confirm the job is completed to your satisfaction. You're fully protected at every step.
Can I join as a skilled worker?
Absolutely! Click "Join Platform", create your profile, complete the verification process, and start receiving job requests from clients near you. It's completely free to join.
What if I'm not happy with the work?
Our satisfaction guarantee means if you're unhappy with the quality of work, you can raise a dispute and our support team will mediate. You can request a redo or a full refund.
